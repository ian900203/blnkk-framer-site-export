Buyer Questions

FAQ for the first public version.

What can BLNKK source?

Electronics, subsystems, EMS and PCBA, batteries, RF, mechanical parts, and manufacturing capabilities where Taiwan suppliers are a strong fit.

How are suppliers verified?

The AI gathers signals, then BLNKK reviews ownership, capability fit, public evidence, and sensitivity before introductions.

What happens to confidential details?

Buyer-sensitive fields stay gated until BLNKK approves the disclosure path and the buyer chooses to move forward.

How fast is the first shortlist?

The MVP target is first signals within 48 hours after the brief is complete, with deeper verification available as a paid review.

Is this Taiwan-only?

Taiwan is the starting wedge. The same workflow can expand to trusted allied supplier markets as the dataset grows.

What happens after the shortlist?

The buyer chooses who they want to meet. BLNKK reviews the path and helps turn qualified matches into supplier conversations.
